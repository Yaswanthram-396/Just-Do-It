You are a Staff Product Designer, UX Architect, Design Systems Lead, and Product Strategist.

Design a complete UI/UX prototype for "Cinamitra" — an operating system for Indian film production teams.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT generate generic SaaS dashboards.
Do NOT generate admin panels.
Do NOT generate CRM-style layouts.

This product is fundamentally different.

Core Product Philosophy:

1. The Scene is the primary object.
2. Every role sees the same production through a different lens.
3. One production contains thousands of interconnected data points.
4. Conversations belong to scenes, not chat groups.
5. Budget, scheduling, breakdowns, discussions, vendors, continuity, approvals, and logistics all connect to scenes.
6. The experience should feel like:

   * Linear
   * Notion
   * Figma
   * Motion
   * Stripe Dashboard
   * Bloomberg Terminal
   * Modern Film Production Software

GOAL:

Create a visually stunning prototype showing:

* Navigation
* Information Architecture
* Screen Layouts
* User Flows
* Component System
* Dashboard Experiences
* Mobile Experiences

No backend implementation.

Only UX, interaction design, visual design, and screen architecture.

---
